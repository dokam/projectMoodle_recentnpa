Welcome to Moodle of Recent project

The RECENT Project is a Northern Periphery and Arctic Interreg Programme which aims to increase energy knowledge in rural communities. It will help them to have more resilient and energy efficient public infrastructure capable of handling climate change related risks. This virtual learning campus offers a knowledge exchange programme from successful pilots that can be used by communities across the European Union.
